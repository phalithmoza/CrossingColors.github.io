Crossing Colors

The colors are bright,
Drowning me in deep,
Taking away my sight,
Colors singing me to sleep.

Everything is bright,
So many lovely days,
the words I liked to write,
now burning in a blaze.

Red is the shining,
across all the roses,
Drawn with silver lining,
It's the darkness it closes.

The sweet orange I see,
Color of the maturing sun,
Seems to pour into me,
that left me undone.

Yellow of the sunflower,
Warming me inside,
Creating great power,
Making me abide.

Green of mother nature,
Making everything lovely,
Like a kind stranger,
With the magic of recovery.

The calmness of blue,
Comforting Sky and Ocean,
So endless to my view,
So many words left unspoken.

the indigo moments,
So soft and tender,
It felt so stolen,
But well remembered.

Ultraviolet always glows,
throughout the dark,
Killing the shadows,
And leaving a spark.

The colors are running,
Through the seams,
Everything is shining,
Colors making a stream.

I let myself flow,
Now loved and lost,
On me the colors grow,
All of them I have crossed.
